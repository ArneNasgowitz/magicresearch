This is the investment task you know from the practice round. You start this task with an amount of 4 Rand. You can distribute this endowment between two accounts, the cash account and the investment account. Please note that your decision will affect your bonus payment!

The table at the bottom of the page gives you an overview over the five different choices that you have.

You have 4 Rand. How much do you want to place in the Cash account and how much in the Investment account?

Cash Account

Every Rand you pay into the cash account will be directly transfered to your payment. There is no risk and no interest.

{% formfield player.invest_cash_main %}
Investment Account

For every Rand you pay into the investment account, there is a 50% probability that this is trippled, and a 50% probability that you will lose your investment.

{% formfield player.invest_inv_main %}

The two Return-columns give you the payoff in the lucky case (when your investment is trippled) and the unlucky case (when you lose your investment).
