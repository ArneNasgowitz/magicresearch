Hello and thank you for choosing to work on this study. Please carefully read the instructions below, before you start. Your payment will consist of a wage of 5 Rand and a bonus payment that will depend on your performance in two main tasks. This bonus payment will in most cases be higher than the wage.

This study will consist of three parts:

Practice round

Here you will get familiar with the two main tasks in the experiment, as well as the payment scheme. Paying close attention will help you to maximize your payment.

Main round

Here you will work on two tasks that will affect your bonus payment and write a short essay.

Survey round

Finally we will ask you some questions related to the experiment.

Note that it is mandatory to complete all tasks in order to receive any payment. We estimate that this study will take you around 15 minutes and ask you to complete it without taking any breaks
